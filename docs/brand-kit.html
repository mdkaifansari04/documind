8. Other Brand Rules

Spacing, shape, motion, and tone

Voice & Tone

  • Direct, technical, and concise.
  • Avoid hype-heavy claims; lead with clear value.
  • Use plain language for workflows and outcomes.

Shape Language

  • Primary radius: 0.5rem for UI components.
  • Cards: 10px to 16px corner radius for marketing layouts.
  • Use subtle borders over heavy outlines.

Motion

  • Short, smooth transitions (150ms to 220ms).
  • Prefer fade/slide over bouncy effects.
  • Highlight state changes, not decoration.
